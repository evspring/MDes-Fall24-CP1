A webpage about building keyboards!

Building keyboards can be a difficult process but it becomes a lot easier if you know the parts.

There are a few major parts you'll need for building any keyboard, the first of which is a motherboard, shown below. This is the part of making sure your keyboard works and registers inputs.

The next major part is the case, which will hold everything together.

The third part you'll need is the switches, which will register inputs on the keyboard.

And the final major part you'll need are your keycaps. These can make your keyboard look, sound and feel different and are highly customizable.

With all these parts you'll be able to build any kind of keyboard you'd like with full customizability to the shape, feel, color, and sound of the keyboard. There are a ton of options to choose from.
